Defining the resource naming strategy :

we are going to use the slash for resources pattern path because of the amount of data generated by sensors and different captures and the hash # for ontological terms paths .

The domain of the generated Dataset is:
 http://www.smart-city.GP-LDATA.org/

Ontological terms path: 
http://www.smart-city.GP-LDATA.org/city/ontology/Road-traffic-accidents#

•Ontological terms pattern: 
http://www.smart-city.GP-LDATA.org/city/ontology/Road-traffic-accidents#<term_name>
Example: http://www.smart-city.GP-LDATA.org/Leeds/ontology/Road-traffic-accidents#hasRefNumber
	http://www.smart-city.GP-LDATA.org/Leeds/ontology/Road-traffic-accidents#hasNumberOfVehicles

Individuals path: 
http://www.smart-city.GP-LDATA.org/city/resource/


•Individuals pattern: 
http://www.smart-city.GP-LDATA.org/city/resource/<resource_type>/<resource_name>
-Example: http://www.smart-city.GP-LDATA.org/Leeds/resource/Date/AccidentDate
	  http://www.smart-city.GP-LDATA.org/Leeds/resource/string/WeatherConditions
